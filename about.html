Welcome

The society A M L I was formed primarily to promote higher educational institutions in kerala. The primary purpose is to provide opportunities for higher education for deserving students. To begin with it is proposed to start a Polytechnic College, the society has ambitious plans, and it now wishes to concentrate on setting up a polytechnic college. Technical education in India has grown explosively in the last few years. The area of our college compound is 4.25 acres and having a vast area of Administrative, amenities and instructional.
