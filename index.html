CIVIL ENGINEERING

A Diploma in Civil Engineering is a three-year program focused on providing students with a comprehensive understanding of civil infrastructure and construction principles. The curriculum covers subjects such as structural design, surveying, materials testing, and project management. Through a combination of theoretical learning and practical experiences, graduates are prepared for entry-level roles in the construction industry.

MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

A three-year program, a Diploma in Mechanical Engineering emphasizes core concepts like thermodynamics, mechanics, and materials science. Students gain hands-on experience in manufacturing processes, machine design, and maintenance. Upon completion, graduates are ready for entry-level roles in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and energy.

Focused on automotive technology, this three-year diploma program covers topics like vehicle design, engine mechanics, and automotive electronics. Students acquire practical skills in diagnostics, repair, and maintenance of automobiles, preparing them for careers in the automotive industry as technicians, service engineers, or quality control professionals.
